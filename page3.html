Why should we care about design in the disinformation ecosystem?

Design is a quiet but central player in—well—everything! Most people don't recognize that their actions are being mediated through design because we don't always see that it's there (until it breaks or is frustrating to navigate, but that would be bad design!).

There are two key ways of understanding a design object: the architectural model and the affordance model.
